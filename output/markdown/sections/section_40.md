|  |  _Explanation 2.—_ For the removal of doubts, it is hereby declared that for the purposes of this sub-clause, any sum paid on account of any rate or tax levied includes any sum eligible for relief of tax under [section 90A](javascript:ShowMainContent\('Act', 'CMSID', '102120000000090981', ''\);).
