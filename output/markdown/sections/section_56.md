_F.—Income from other sources_

**Income from other sources.**

**56.** (1) Income of every kind which is not to be excluded from the total income under this Act shall be chargeable to income-tax under the head "Income from other sources", if it is not chargeable to income-tax under any of the heads specified in [section 14](javascript:ShowMainContent\('Act', 'CMSID', '102120000000090738', ''\);), items _A_ to _E_.
