**ADVANCE RULINGS**

**44E.**[70](javascript:ShowFootnote\('fn270'\);)[(1) An application for obtaining an advance ruling under sub-section (1) of section 245Q shall be made [71](javascript:ShowFootnote\('fn271'\);)[***],—

(_a_)|  | in Form No. 34C in respect of a non-resident applicant referred to in sub-clause (_i_) of clause (_a_) of section 245N;  
---|---|---  
(_b_)|  | in Form No. 34D in respect of a resident applicant referred to in sub-clause (_ii_) of clause (_a_) of section 245N seeking advance ruling in relation to a transaction undertaken or proposed to be undertaken by him with a non-resident; and  
(_c_)|  | in Form No. 34DA in respect of a resident applicant referred to in sub-clause (_iia_) of clause (_a_) of section 245N falling within any such class or category of person as notified by the Central Government in exercise of the powers conferred by [72](javascript:ShowFootnote\('fn272'\);)[item (_III_) of sub-clause (_A_) of clause (_b_)] of that section;  
(_d_)|  | in Form No. 34E in respect of a resident falling within any such class or category of person as notified by the Central Government in exercise of the powers conferred by [73](javascript:ShowFootnote\('fn273'\);)[item (_IV_) of sub-clause (_A_) of clause (_b_)] of section 245N; and  
(_e_)|  | in Form No. 34EA, in respect of an applicant referred to in [74](javascript:ShowFootnote\('fn274'\);)[item (_V_) of sub-clause (_A_) of clause (_b_)] of section 245N of the Act,  
  
and shall be verified in the manner indicated therein.]
